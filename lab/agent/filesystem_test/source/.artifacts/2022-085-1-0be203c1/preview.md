## Summary

### 1. Initial Assessment and Emergency Declaration
On **February 10, 2022**, a technical inspection (**SEI 6041.202210000225-1**) was conducted by **SIURB/Obras** at **Av. Caititu**, **Cidade Antônio Estêvão de Carvalho - SUB-IT**, focusing on the **Jacupeval stream**. The inspection, documented in a photographic report (**058621333**) and a technical report (**058621336**), revealed significant compromise of the stream banks in two sections of **Av. Caititu**. This led to the undermining of existing roadways on both sides and damage to the drainage system, posing risks to pedestrians and vehicles, as well as to nearby structures and utility networks (electrical, water, sewage). The situation was deemed calamitous, requiring urgent intervention to prevent further damage and ensure public safety.

### 2. Contracting Process
Given the emergency, **SIURB/OBRAS** recommended immediate intervention. Three companies were consulted for the emergency work: **FFL Sinalização, Comércio e Serviços Eireli**, **MENG Engenharia Comércio e Indústria Ltda**, and **ARC – Comércio Construção e Administração de Serviços Ltda**.
*   **FFL Sinalização, Comércio e Serviços Eireli** (CNPJ: **08.068.681/0001-31**) expressed interest in executing the work in response to **Ofício nº 54/SIURB/OBRAS - G/2022** and offered a **B.D.I. of 28%**.
*   **ARC - Comercio Construção e Administração de Serviços Ltda** (CNPJ: **01.565.706/0001-63**) responded to **Ofício nº 55/SIURB/OBRAS - G/2022** and proposed a **BDI of 28.90%**.
*   **MENG Engenharia Comércio e Indústria Ltda** was also consulted via **Ofício nº 56/SIURB/OBRAS - G/2022**.

**FFL Sinalização, Comércio e Serviços Eireli** was selected due to its capacity for such works, availability of equipment and personnel for immediate start, and a more favorable BDI. The contract was to be based on **SIURB**'s unit cost table from **July 2021**, with a maximum duration of **180 days**, under **Article 24, Inciso IV of Federal Law 8.666/93**.

### 3. Authorization and Project Details
On **February 11, 2022**, an **Order to Start Work (SEI nº 058621349)** was issued to **FFL Sinalização, Comércio e Serviços Eireli**, with **Eng. Osmar Dias Dos Santos** designated as the fiscal engineer. The work was to commence on **February 11, 2022**, and last **180 days**.

On **March 16, 2022**, **SIURB/ATAJ** issued a legal opinion (**060025969**) supporting the emergency contracting, citing the imminent risk and the applicability of **Article 24, Inciso IV, of Law nº 8.666/93**. The opinion noted that the **Order to Start Work** was issued on **February 11, 2022**, but the legal review was only conducted on **March 16, 2022**. It recommended investigating the functional responsibility for this delay.

On **March 16, 2022**, **Marcos Monteiro**, the Secretary, authorized the emergency contract with **FFL Sinalização, Comércio e Serviços Eireli** (**CNPJ 08.068.681/0001-31**) for slope containment and pavement recomposition at **Córrego Jacupeval**, **Av. Caititu**. This authorization (**060026581**) was published in the **Diário Oficial da Cidade de São Paulo** on **March 18, 2022**.

The project involves:
*   **Geotechnical Surveys:** Conducted by **CAP Engenharia e Geotecnia LTDA** from **February 24 to March 8, 2022**, at **Av. Caititu**, **Cidade Antonio Estêvão de Carvalho**, São Paulo. Nine boreholes (**SP-01 to SP-09**) were performed using the SPT method (NBR 6484/2020). The soil profiles generally consist of sandy clay (superficial/fill), plastic sandy clay (alluvial), and mature residual sandy clay. Impenetrable layers were encountered at various depths.
*   **Work Scope:** Slope containment and pavement recomposition.
*   **Technical Specifications:** Detailed guidelines for superficial drainage, including elements like catch basins, manholes, connections, storm sewers, passage boxes, curbs, gutters, and crossings. It also specifies project parameters for circular galleries (minimum diameter **0.60m**, max velocity **5.0m/s**, min velocity **0.60m/s**), pavement work (PMSP/SP norms from **October 1992**), and geotechnical investigations (minimum **2 boreholes** per work, SPT tests, and a specialist report).
*   **Executive Guidelines:** Procedures for site location, terrain clearing, excavation (common and with explosives), provisional and definitive superficial drainage (gutters, concrete gutters, gabion downspouts, step downspouts, impact blocks), and storm sewer pipe installation (concrete pipes, NBR 9793 and 9794, specific classes for different uses).
