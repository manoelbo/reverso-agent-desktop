## Summary

### 1. Initial Inspection and Emergency Declaration
On **August 2, 2021**, an inspection was conducted by **SIURB/OBRAS** in response to **SEI 6060.2020/0002450-6** regarding an emergency work process **SEI 6022.2021/0001924-8** at **Rua Ilha dos Papagaios x Rua Girassol Miúdo**, Vila Industrial, São Paulo. The inspection, led by **Osmar Dias Dos Santos** (CREA Ro5060252882/D), identified a worsening landslide on the left bank of the **Córrego Água Vermelha**. This caused partial collapse of the sidewalk, curbs, gutters, and drainage system, creating negative slope points that endanger pedestrians. The report noted evolving landslide wedges, risking the existing road and nearby structures, including electrical installations, water supply, and sewage systems. The soil, composed of heterogeneous landfill materials (debris, organic soil, alluvial soil), lacks cohesion and stability. The situation was deemed calamitous, requiring urgent intervention to prevent further damage and ensure public safety.

### 2. Authorization for Emergency Contract
Considering the severe and imminent risk, authorization was requested for the emergency contracting of a specialized company for partial containment works on the banks of the **Córrego Água Vermelha**.
*   On **August 2, 2021**, **Osmar Dias Dos Santos** forwarded the inspection report for further action.
*   On **August 3, 2021**, **Adria Giaccheri** forwarded the photographic report (**SEI 049344487**) and inspection report (**SEI 049344650**) for continuation.
*   On **August 6, 2021**, **Engª Adriana Siano Boggio Biazzi**, Director of the Technical Department, recommended the direct emergency contracting of **ESCOPO CONSTRUTORA EIRELI** (CNPJ **12.965.938/0001-25**). This decision followed consultations with three companies (**TALUDE CONSTRUÇÕES S.A.** and **JRA EMPREENDIMENTOS E ENGENHARIA LTDA.** were also consulted but not selected due to BDI discount). **ESCOPO CONSTRUTORA EIRELI** was chosen due to its capacity, equipment, personnel availability for immediate start, and agreement to a reduced BDI. The execution period was set at **180 days**. This was based on **Federal Law nº 8666/93, article 24, inciso IV**, for emergency situations.
*   On **August 12, 2021**, **Paulo César Nannini**, Municipal Prosecutor, issued a legal opinion (**SEI 050052080**) supporting the emergency contracting, citing the imminent risk and the need to prevent greater damage. He emphasized that the contract should be limited to the minimum necessary to mitigate the risk and that the chosen company must meet fiscal and legal requirements.
*   On **August 12, 2021**, **Marcos Monteiro**, Municipal Secretary of **SIURB**, authorized the emergency contracting of **ESCOPO CONSTRUTORA EIRELI - EPP** for the works, to be completed within **180 days** from the start order (**049871527**). The authorization was published in the **Diário Oficial da Cidade de São Paulo** on **August 14, 2021**.

A **Memorial de Cálculo** (Calculation Report) for the executive project of containment of the creek banks parallel to **Rua Ilha dos Papagaios**, between **Rua Ataulfo Alves** and **Rua Altamisa**, Vila Industrial, São Paulo, was prepared by **Eng. Joel Tsutomo Yamashita** (CREA: **060.173.327-8**) for **MATHESIS ENGENHARIA E CONSTRUÇÃO LTDA.**, with **PLANORTE Engenharia Ltda.** as the contractor.
*   The project aims to contain the creek banks and address the severe landslide.
*   It includes the implementation of a **2.00x2.00m** drainage channel, variable height walls on the left bank for slope stability, and reorganization of the micro-drainage system on **Rua Ilha dos Papagaios**.
*   This involves installing curbs, gutters, paving the currently unpaved section, and a rainwater drainage network with **60cm** diameter pipes to capture runoff from **Rua Girassol Miúdo**, discharging into the creek via a hydraulic staircase.
*   The report details hydrological calculations using the Rational and Modified Rational methods, considering recurrence times of **10, 25, 50, and 100 years** for different sections of the creek.
*   Hydraulic dimensioning of the channels and pipe galleries is based on **PMSP** guidelines, using the continuity equation and Chezy-Manning formula.
*   The project also includes specifications for paving, soil and foundation investigations, and executive guidelines for superficial drainage works, including excavations, temporary and permanent drainage systems, concrete structures, and gabion works.
